# Voice Assistant Command List

Here is a list of commands you can say to your assistant.

## 🧠 Calculations & Knowledge
*   "Calculate [expression]" (e.g., "Calculate 50 plus 50", "Calculate 25 times 15")
*   "What is [topic]?" (e.g., "What is the capital of India?", "Who is Bill Gates?")
*   "Wikipedia [topic]" (e.g., "Wikipedia Artificial Intelligence")

## 📰 Daily Updates
*   "News" (Reads top headlines from Times of India)
*   "The time" (Tells current time)
*   "Weather" (Tells weather - *Note: Requires OpenWeatherMap API key if not set*)

## 📧 Communication
*   "Send a mail" (Follows prompts to send email via Gmail)
*   "Email to Gaurav" (Sends to a pre-set address)
*   "Send message" (Sends SMS - *Requires Twilio*)

## ⚙️ System & Utilities
*   "Open Google"
*   "Open Youtube"
*   "Open StackOverflow"
*   "Play music" (Plays from your Music folder)
*   "Write a note" (Dictate a note to save to jarvis.txt)
*   "Show note" (Reads back your saved notes)
*   "Empty recycle bin"
*   "Lock window"
*   "Shutdown system"
*   "Restart"
*   "Camera" or "Take a photo"

## 💬 Conversation
*   "How are you"
*   "Who made you"
*   "Change name"
*   "Joke" (Tells a random programming joke)
*   "Exit" (Stops the assistant)
